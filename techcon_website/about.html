Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software engineers and has since grown into an international conference attracting thousands of attendees. Over the years, it has become a platform for launching innovations and discussing the future of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, education, and collaboration within the tech community. We strive to empower individuals and organizations by providing a space for idea exchange, professional growth, and community building.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Lin – An AI visionary who spoke in 2021 about ethical machine learning practices and the future of responsible AI development.

John Patel – CTO of InnovateX, shared groundbreaking ideas on cloud computing and global digital infrastructure in 2022.

Elena Ramos – A leading cybersecurity expert who provided practical insights on protecting digital assets in the modern age during her 2023 keynote.
